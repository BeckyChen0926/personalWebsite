Interactive STEM Passport

interactive design | html+css | STEM | feminism

Interactive STEM Passport is a website I created that introduces three under-represented accomplished women in STEM. They fields of specialty range from computer science to biology, but are less known by the public in comparison to male scientists of similar accomplishments.


Team
myself
Time Frame
2 days
My Contribution
Self-taught html and css using online resources; did research on female scientists; built the website from skratch

Problem Statement: Why is this project meaningful?

Because of the fact that the STEM was long "dominated" by men due to oppression of women and stereotypes that "women aren't suitable for science." This project serves as a portfolio to acknowledge the STEM achievement of three women. Meanwhile, there are so much more non-cismale scientists that were erased and ignored from history. I believe it is everyone's responsibility to raise awareness on this situation.


My Approach

To raise awareness on this long-standing inequality and under-representation, I build this website to introduce three female scientists. I chose the form of a website because it could be populated with many information and actively updated. It is an awesome way for people to keep learning about these under-represented scientists.
