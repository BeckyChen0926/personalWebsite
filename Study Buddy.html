Study Buddy Mobile Application

interactive design | needfinding | wireframe | testing

Study Buddy is a mobile app designed for students taking online courses or looking for online studying atmosphere. It aims to help students keep track of their meeting links and course schedules, share study materials among classmates, and provides them a safe platform to study or collaborate online outside of class.


Team
4 team members from CS220
Time Frame
1.5 months in 2022
My Contribution
Attend all meetings; Join force with others to complete each project milestone; Plan general direction and working schedule for our team

Problem Statement: Why is this project meaningful?

Because of the isolated environment created by online learning, there is an inability for students to study and focus together with other students while taking online classes at home. Furthermore, the variety of websites and virtual meeting platforms teachers and professors use makes it difficult for students to meet, collaborate, and study together outside of classes. It is important for students to have a platform on which they can freely and safely do so.


Our Approach

To solve the problem, we believe an app that has many student-friendly features would be useful. Thus, we designed Study Buddy that can both serve as directory and schedulers as well as online study rooms. We chose to design a mobile app that could solve the problem we identified for the current online studying environment. Mobile phones are widely used with user-friendly functions like camera and microphone, which are sufficient for an online study room environment.
