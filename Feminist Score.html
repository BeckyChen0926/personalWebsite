Rating Feminist Scores for Movies

java | problem solving | sorting algorithm | feminism

For this project, we wanted to have a method to sort movies from the most feminist to the least. To accomplish this, we created a program to assign a ‘feminist score’ to movies based on whether they passed or failed various alternative Bechdel tests.


Team
3 team members from CS230
Time Frame
2 weeks in 2021
My Contribution
Attended all meetings; Main coder for the team; Wrote the final report together with the team

Problem Statement: Why is this project meaningful?

Because the movie industry was dominated by men, women were often ignored as characters in movies. Movies that are full of men cannot be considered feminist movies, sometimes even movies full of women cannot be considered so. This project taught me various ways to determine how feminist a movie is based on various standards using my JAVA knowledges. And the result was not surprising-- not many movies can be considered feminist movies.


Our Approach

To solve the problem, we used Java to code a sorting algorithum based on our understanding of queues, sorting, heap, and collections. When we run each movie through the program, a score will be assigned to the movie. Higher the score is, the more feminist it could be. We created a rating standard for each alternative Bachdel test based on their difficulties and importance. We also programmed a tie breaker in case that multiple movies earn the same score, a different testing standard will be employed in addition.
